BANGALORE UNIVERSITY NOTIFICATION (EXAMINATION FEE & DOCUMENTS) FOR I-BHM STUDENTS

As per the revised regulations of Bangalore University which has been communicated vide Circular No. Aca-1/UG.Adm.Appl/2010-11 dated 21st June, 2010, the Examination Fee pertaining to each semester is to be collected at the commencement of the semester and remitted to the University.

Till now, the practice has been to collect the examination fee along with the examination application about a month prior to the commencement of the semester examination.

As per this directive of Bangalore University, you are required to remit the following fee (applicable to the category you belong to) at the time of admission along with the fees payable as communicated earlier.

1KARNATAKA PUC BOARD Rs.1945/-
2CBSE/ ISEC & NON KARNATAKA BOARDS Rs.3265/-
3INDIAN STUDENTS WHO HAVE PASSED QUALIFYING EXAMINATION FROM OTHER COUNTRIES Rs.5650/-
4FOREIGN STUDENTS Rs.6265/-

Documents (in original + 2 photocopies attested by the Principal of the Institution last attended) to be submitted at the time of admission

> FOR CANDIDATES WITHIN KARNATAKA

  • a. Application in the form of OMR (will be provided by College)
  • b. Original Marks Card - SSLC / 10th Std AND 1st and 2nd PUC
  • c. ICSE/ CBSC 11th and 12th Standard Original Marks Card
  • d. Transfer Certificate
  • e. Caste Certificate and Income Certificate wherever applicable

> FOR CANDIDATES OUTSIDE KARNATAKA

  • All the above documents +
  • f. Migration Certificate

> FOR CANDIDATES FROM OUTSIDE INDIA

  • All the above documents +
  • g. Original Passport +
  • h. Student Visa +
  • i. Eligibility Certificate Issued by Bangalore University

You are required to bring all the above documents (as applicable)
